STORY OF KEY NATION

KEY NATION The main body of Key Nation company is to make animation peripheral products, and the combination of interesting designing and animation elements is our passion.In this process, the boss came up with the idea of designing a better mechanical keyboard of his own because of the boring design of other keyboards. In 2020, we have taken action for this and have a very experienced keyboard design and production team.

It has been 500 years since the keyboard design of Key Nation series, and the will launch the second generation products. We have been thinking about how to combine product design with animation elements, which goes beyond printing patterns on packages.

Despite this process is full of unknowns, but it's fascinating and happy enough. Maybe anime design and mechinacal keyboard will exist independently, or they will be perfectly integrated, and we can always find inspiration in this journey.
Key Nation mechanical keyboard series is not just a job for us, it's a passion and a dream, and we will continue to do this.
